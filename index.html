Welcome to Zen dotline ZEN OFFSET Private Limited (ZOPL) was established in 1997 in Mumbai by its founder chairman Mr Chandrakant Mehta, a printing professional turned entrepreneur. Over the years, the company has remained true to its founding principles: to Print on variable surface other than paper, and to give new attractive product. and has invest in the best systems and equipments, partnering with clients, quality control and a passion for the unexplored possibilities of print. Today ZOPL is highly recommended Speciality Printers in Mumbai, with 2 unit , one in Mumbai-Ghatkopar and other in Bhiwandi company serving many industries with variety of products.
